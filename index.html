About

Jeremy You is a 2017 graduate from the Fashion Institute of Technology. He recieved a BFA in fashion design with a concentration in special occasion and eveningwear and his garments have been featured in FIT's 2015 'Sculpture' exhibit, the 2017 FADC runway show and the 2017 Future of Fashion show.

Jeremy is currently working as a production coordinator at the world renowned J Mendel atelier in New York City, and has previously worked with the Vera Wang design teams. He is currently pursuing a web development certificate from the Columbia School of Engineering with the hopes of combining fashion and coding to further advance his career.

Outside of work and school, Jeremy is an avid dog enthusiast and is the proud dog daddy to a corgi and a schnoodle. He also enjoys food; especially desserts, travel, and a good cocktail. On his days off he can often be found shopping or enjoying a relaxing walk on the beach or a trail.
